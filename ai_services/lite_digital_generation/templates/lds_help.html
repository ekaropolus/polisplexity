App Context

The Polisplexity app is designed to help you create better cities by utilizing AI and urban models based on complex dynamic systems. It provides a 3D visualization of cities where you can explore buildings, lamps, trees, and more. Follow the instructions below to make the most out of the app:

  1. Choose a city from the dropdown menu in the navigation bar.
  2. The 3D view of the selected city will be displayed in the City View card.
  3. Explore the city by using your mouse to rotate and zoom in/out.
  4. Click on any building, lamp, or tree to view additional details.
  5. Use the search box to ask questions or search for specific information about the city.
  6. View the statistics and status summary in the Statistics card.
  7. Use the Create Objects card to add new buildings, lamps, and trees to the city.
  8. Click the "View in VR" link in the City View card to experience the city in virtual reality.
  9. Enjoy exploring the virtual city!
